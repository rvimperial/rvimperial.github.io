February Updates

Repair of Pipelines near Block 1 Lot 15 & Block 4 Lot 5

We've fixed 2 pipeline leaks near Block 1 Lot 15 and Block 4 Lot 5, affecting our community's water lines. These leaks were billed to the community water meter, not individual meters. Hopefully, this will lower our next water bill.

The repair will be done tomorrow, Feb 20. We've discussed hiring an expert to check for hidden leaks and reduce our shared water costs.

Sorry for the inconvenience. If you spot any leaks, please report ASAP so we can fix them quickly and save on water.

Thanks for your understanding and cooperation!
